可以。我们从一段“外部文本”开始，把你的思路完整捋一遍。

你的目标不是做普通问答，而是：

> **从外部文本中抽取变量—机制—条件—目标变量关系，构建图谱；再用图谱检索和组合规则，生成当前任务需要的规则集合。**

这正好可以替代 ExoLLM 里人工写死的自然属性 prompt。你给的源码中，`T (degC)`、`rh (%)`、`wv (m/s)` 等变量的属性描述都是人工写在 `nature_attribute_template` 里的，然后再和固定的 trend、period、stability、noise 模板拼接成 prompt。

---

## 例子：从一段外部文本开始

假设我们从一篇气象/生态相关文档中获得这样一段文本：

```text
Air temperature influences atmospheric CO2 concentration through two major biological processes. 
During daytime, higher temperature can enhance photosynthetic activity when sufficient radiation and vegetation are present, which may reduce local CO2 concentration. 
At night or under respiration-dominant conditions, higher temperature can increase plant and soil respiration, which may release more CO2 into the atmosphere. 
Wind speed can further affect CO2 concentration by changing atmospheric mixing and dispersion, especially under unstable boundary-layer conditions.
```

这段文本里有几个关键信息：

- 变量：`air temperature`, `wind speed`
    
- 目标变量：`atmospheric CO2 concentration`
    
- 机制：`photosynthetic activity`, `plant and soil respiration`, `atmospheric mixing and dispersion`
    
- 条件：`daytime`, `sufficient radiation`, `vegetation present`, `night`, `respiration-dominant conditions`, `unstable boundary-layer conditions`
    

---

# Step 1：证据句切分

先不要直接让 LLM 生成 prompt，而是把文本拆成 evidence sentences。

```text
E1: Air temperature influences atmospheric CO2 concentration through two major biological processes.

E2: During daytime, higher temperature can enhance photosynthetic activity when sufficient radiation and vegetation are present, which may reduce local CO2 concentration.

E3: At night or under respiration-dominant conditions, higher temperature can increase plant and soil respiration, which may release more CO2 into the atmosphere.

E4: Wind speed can further affect CO2 concentration by changing atmospheric mixing and dispersion, especially under unstable boundary-layer conditions.
```

每个 evidence sentence 后面都要保留来源，比如论文标题、章节、URL、段落编号。这样后续规则不是“LLM 编的”，而是“有证据支撑的”。

---

# Step 2：抽取 VMCT 规则候选

然后把每个 evidence sentence 转成你的核心 schema：

```text
Variable — Mechanism — Condition — Target
```

从 E2 可以抽出：

```json
{
  "variable": "air temperature",
  "mechanism": "photosynthetic activity",
  "condition": "daytime with sufficient radiation and vegetation",
  "target": "atmospheric CO2 concentration",
  "effect": "may reduce",
  "evidence": "During daytime, higher temperature can enhance photosynthetic activity when sufficient radiation and vegetation are present, which may reduce local CO2 concentration."
}
```

从 E3 可以抽出：

```json
{
  "variable": "air temperature",
  "mechanism": "plant and soil respiration",
  "condition": "night or respiration-dominant conditions",
  "target": "atmospheric CO2 concentration",
  "effect": "may increase",
  "evidence": "At night or under respiration-dominant conditions, higher temperature can increase plant and soil respiration, which may release more CO2 into the atmosphere."
}
```

从 E4 可以抽出：

```json
{
  "variable": "wind speed",
  "mechanism": "atmospheric mixing and dispersion",
  "condition": "unstable boundary-layer conditions",
  "target": "CO2 concentration",
  "effect": "may affect",
  "evidence": "Wind speed can further affect CO2 concentration by changing atmospheric mixing and dispersion, especially under unstable boundary-layer conditions."
}
```

这里有一个重点：  
**同一个变量 temperature 可以通过不同机制产生不同方向的影响。**

所以不能简单抽成：

```text
temperature → affects → CO2
```

而应该抽成：

```text
temperature → photosynthesis → CO2 decrease
temperature → respiration → CO2 increase
```

这就是你引入 **机制** 和 **条件** 的价值。

---

# Step 3：实体对齐到数据集变量

外部文本里写的是：

```text
air temperature
wind speed
atmospheric CO2 concentration
```

但是你的数据集里可能叫：

```text
T (degC)
wv (m/s)
CO2 (ppm)
```

所以需要做 entity linking：

```text
air temperature → T (degC)
wind speed → wv (m/s)
atmospheric CO2 concentration → CO2 (ppm)
```

对齐之后，规则变成和数据集绑定的形式：

```json
{
  "variable": "T (degC)",
  "standard_variable": "air temperature",
  "mechanism": "photosynthetic activity",
  "condition": "daytime with sufficient radiation and vegetation",
  "target": "CO2 (ppm)",
  "effect": "may reduce"
}
```

这一步非常重要。否则你的外部知识库和实际数据列名是脱节的。

---

# Step 4：构建 VMCT 图谱

现在把这些规则放进图谱。

节点包括：

```text
Variable:
- T (degC)
- wv (m/s)

Mechanism:
- photosynthetic activity
- plant and soil respiration
- atmospheric mixing and dispersion

Condition:
- daytime
- sufficient radiation
- vegetation present
- night
- respiration-dominant condition
- unstable boundary-layer condition

Target:
- CO2 (ppm)

Evidence:
- E2
- E3
- E4
```

边包括：

```text
T (degC) --activates_under_condition--> photosynthetic activity
photosynthetic activity --decreases--> CO2 (ppm)
daytime --enables--> photosynthetic activity
sufficient radiation --enables--> photosynthetic activity
vegetation present --enables--> photosynthetic activity
E2 --supports--> this path
```

另一条路径：

```text
T (degC) --enhances_under_condition--> plant and soil respiration
plant and soil respiration --increases--> CO2 (ppm)
night --enables--> plant and soil respiration
respiration-dominant condition --enables--> plant and soil respiration
E3 --supports--> this path
```

再一条路径：

```text
wv (m/s) --changes--> atmospheric mixing and dispersion
atmospheric mixing and dispersion --affects--> CO2 (ppm)
unstable boundary-layer condition --modulates--> atmospheric mixing and dispersion
E4 --supports--> this path
```

这时图谱不是最终产物，而是一个**规则检索和规则组合的中间结构**。

---

# Step 5：给定当前任务，做图谱检索

假设当前任务是：

```json
{
  "target": "CO2 (ppm)",
  "exogenous_variables": ["T (degC)", "wv (m/s)", "rh (%)", "PAR (mol/m/s)"],
  "task": "forecasting or visual explanation",
  "data_context": {
    "period": "daily",
    "time_resolution": "10 minutes"
  }
}
```

系统从图谱中检索：

```text
T (degC) → mechanism → CO2 (ppm)
wv (m/s) → mechanism → CO2 (ppm)
PAR (mol/m/s) → mechanism → CO2 (ppm)
rh (%) → mechanism → CO2 (ppm)
```

对于 `T (degC)`，图谱返回两条高质量路径：

```text
Path 1:
T (degC) → photosynthetic activity → CO2 (ppm)
Condition: daytime + sufficient radiation + vegetation

Path 2:
T (degC) → plant and soil respiration → CO2 (ppm)
Condition: night or respiration-dominant condition
```

对于 `wv (m/s)`，返回：

```text
Path 3:
wv (m/s) → atmospheric mixing and dispersion → CO2 (ppm)
Condition: unstable boundary-layer condition
```

这和普通 RAG 不一样。普通 RAG 只返回几段文本；你的图谱检索返回的是**结构化路径**。

---

# Step 6：由路径生成候选规则

把检索到的路径转成候选规则。

```json
[
  {
    "variable": "T (degC)",
    "mechanism": "photosynthetic activity",
    "condition": "daytime with sufficient radiation and vegetation",
    "target": "CO2 (ppm)",
    "effect": "may reduce",
    "evidence": "E2"
  },
  {
    "variable": "T (degC)",
    "mechanism": "plant and soil respiration",
    "condition": "night or respiration-dominant conditions",
    "target": "CO2 (ppm)",
    "effect": "may increase",
    "evidence": "E3"
  },
  {
    "variable": "wv (m/s)",
    "mechanism": "atmospheric mixing and dispersion",
    "condition": "unstable boundary-layer conditions",
    "target": "CO2 (ppm)",
    "effect": "may affect",
    "evidence": "E4"
  }
]
```

注意这里还不是最终规则集合。因为候选规则可能太多、重复、冲突、证据弱。

---

# Step 7：规则集合优化

这一步是你的关键设计点。

你不是简单取 top-k，而是要生成一个好的 rule set。一个好的规则集合应该满足：

```text
1. 覆盖多个变量
2. 覆盖多个机制
3. 保留条件差异
4. 去掉重复表达
5. 保留证据强的规则
6. 对冲突规则进行条件化处理
```

例如 temperature 有两条规则，看起来方向相反：

```text
temperature → photosynthesis → CO2 decrease
temperature → respiration → CO2 increase
```

不能删掉其中一条，因为它们条件不同。应该保留为：

```text
Rule A:
Under daytime, sufficient radiation, and vegetation-active conditions, T (degC) may reduce CO2 (ppm) through photosynthetic activity.

Rule B:
Under nighttime or respiration-dominant conditions, T (degC) may increase CO2 (ppm) through plant and soil respiration.
```

这就是 **condition** 字段的价值：  
它让冲突关系变成条件化规则，而不是简单互相否定。

---

# Step 8：生成 Prompt-ready 规则

最后把结构化规则转成可以喂给 ExoLLM 或你的可视化模型的 prompt。

最终输出可能是：

```text
The exogenous variable T (degC), representing air temperature, may influence the endogenous variable CO2 (ppm) through photosynthetic activity. Under daytime conditions with sufficient radiation and active vegetation, higher temperature may enhance photosynthesis and reduce local CO2 concentration.

The exogenous variable T (degC) may also influence CO2 (ppm) through plant and soil respiration. Under nighttime or respiration-dominant conditions, higher temperature may increase respiratory CO2 release.

The exogenous variable wv (m/s), representing wind speed, may affect CO2 (ppm) through atmospheric mixing and dispersion. This rule is especially relevant under unstable boundary-layer or wind-driven transport conditions.
```

这就可以替代 ExoLLM 源码里手写的：

```text
Exogenous T (degC) directly interacts with CO2 (ppm) by altering plant respiration and carbon exchange rates.
```

你的版本比原方法多了三点：

1. **有外部证据来源**；
    
2. **区分不同机制**；
    
3. **保留适用条件**。
    

---

# 整个思路串起来

用一句完整的话说：

> 我们先从外部文本中抽取证据句，再将证据句转化为变量—机制—条件—目标变量规则；随后把规则组织成图谱，使变量、机制、条件、目标和证据之间形成可检索路径；当面对一个具体数据集或可视分析任务时，我们从图谱中检索与当前变量和目标变量相关的路径，并将这些路径组合、去重和条件化，最终生成一个有证据支撑的规则集合，用于替代人工写死的多粒度 prompt。

可以画成流程：

```text
External Text
    ↓
Evidence Sentences
    ↓
VMCT Extraction
    ↓
Entity Linking
    ↓
VMCT Graph
    ↓
Task-aware Graph Retrieval
    ↓
Candidate Rules
    ↓
Rule Set Optimization
    ↓
Prompt-ready Rule Set
```

# 对应到你的论文贡献

你可以这样表达你的方法创新：

```text
ExoLLM:
manual templates → prompt set

Your method:
external text → evidence → VMCT graph → graph retrieval → rule set → prompt set
```

这就不是“我用 LLM 生成 prompt”，而是：

> **我用图谱作为中间结构，从外部文本中生成面向当前变量和任务的规则集合。**

这个表述会更稳，也更容易和 GraphRAG、知识图谱、可解释 prompt construction 联系起来。
